About RFI GeoNode

The RFI GeoNode (or Request for Imagery/Information GeoNode) is a digital service provided by the U.S. Department of State, Humanitarian Information Unit (HIU) to coordinate and validate requests by interagency and external partners to the U.S. Government for imagery and information.
