RFI GeoNode / About

The RFI GeoNode (or Request for Imagery/Information GeoNode) is a digital service provided by the U.S. Department of State, Humanitarian Information Unit (HIU) to coordinate and validate requests by interagency and external partners to the U.S. Government for imagery and information.

MapGive / Imagery to the Crowd is a flagship initiative of the U.S. Department of State's Open Government Plan. Through the Imagery to the Crowd (IttC), HIU provides high-resolution commercial satellite imagery services in a format that the volunteer mapping community can use to create open geographic data.
